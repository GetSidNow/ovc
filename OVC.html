Beach Access

Just steps away from your room—hello paradise!

  • Umbrellas & Loungers: Already set up daily—no need to reserve in advance.
  • Beach Towels: Available on the beach deck.
  • If they run out, simply let the Front Desk or the beach butler know and we'll restock right away.
  • Beach Butler Service: While you're relaxing on the sand, feel free to order lunch or drinks through our beach butler—he'll bring it right to you, so you don't have to lift a finger.
  • Note: No lifeguard on duty—please swim responsibly.

Getting Around the Island

Harbour Island is approximately 3 miles long by 1/2 a mile wide so everything is walkable. However, the primary mode of transportation are golf carts.

If you'd like, we can reserve a golf cart for you - just let us know!

Taxis are available but in short supply and require cash only.
